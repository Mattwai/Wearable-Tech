Smart Clothing
Clothing that monitors the wearer's physical condition. Smart shirts and body suits provide biometric data, such as pulse rate, temperature, muscle stretch, heart rhythm and physical movement, that is transmitted via Bluetooth to an app in real time.

Smart Watches
A Smart Watch is a mobile device with a touchscreen display, designed to be worn on the wrist.

Head-mounted Displays
A head-mounted display is a display device, worn on the head or as part of a helmet, that has a small display optic in front of one or each eye.
